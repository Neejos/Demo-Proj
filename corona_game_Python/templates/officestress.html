Office goers, listen up! 5 reasons you should use a stress ball!


Health benefits of stress balls

Stress is a chronic problem which all of us are bound to experience. After fidget spinners and bracelets, stress balls are the new favourite gadget people like to use to fight stress, reduce tension and also, in some ways, help in anger management. Just about big enough to fit in your palm, stress balls are easily available and can be used by anyone. In fact, you can even make them yourself! Apart from stress, these squishy balls can also come in handy in your day-to-day routine to improve your health and help you stay in the best of shape. Here are some benefits you should know about:

Acts as a distraction device

When you need a daily form of diversion in your routine to detox and refresh, using a stress ball can come in very handy. It can help you concentrate, relax and relieve unnecessary tension and cut away the clutter of noises. This is also something workaholics can use in the office. It will make a difference in your productivity!

Improves nervous system

A lot of nerves located in and around our wrists and hands are connected directly to the brain. When you exercise pressure on a stress ball, the nerves and the muscles stimulate and contract, making them stronger. This, in turn, improves nervous system functioning and reduces essential hormones, which control and curb stress levels.

​Mini workout for the body

Using a stress ball not just exercises the stressed muscles around your wrist area and your arm, but gives you a mini workout of sorts. Since the nerves in the body are connected to one another. This is stimulating nerves of the entire body which means that the whole body is exercised.

Improves the quality of life

Living stress and tension free life is an important precedent to promote wellness and enjoy the quality of life. Using stress balls on a regular basis alleviates stress levels, sometimes even works faster than meditation to show benefits. Using a stress ball is one of the natural and cheapest available remedies to fight stress. When you are less stressed, manage everyday tensions in an effective way, your heart stays healthy and sleep better than before, promising you a good life.
